About SurfBot

SurfBot is a discord bot that can do alot of things. It can play music, track levels, auto moderation system,
audit logs, and reaction roles with many more things coming soon.

About Us

We are just a small group of teenage kids who decided they wanted to make a disocrd bot.
We wanted to make the bot free to use since so many bots you have to pay to use it.
When we first started we thought it would be easy but boy were we wrong.
The music part of the bot was when we realized how hard it actually was going to be.
It took us about a month to just get the music function to work and it still gives us problems to this day.
